# ===========================================

To optimize HVAC systems in residential buildings, maintain a setpoint of 20°C in winter and 25°C in summer.

Heat pumps are 2-3 times more efficient than traditional electric resistance heating.

Variable speed HVAC motors consume 50-75% less energy than single-speed motors.
Ceiling fans can make a room feel 4°C cooler, allowing higher AC setpoints and 4-8% energy savings.
Night setback (lowering temperature at night) saves 5-10% on heating costs annually.

# ===========================================
